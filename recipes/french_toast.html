French Toast


French toats, a non-french plate widely known across the world, that for many might be a very simple plate, yet a really delicious aperient that you can have at dinner, similar to pancakes, but having its own and distinctive texture and flavour


Have you ever wanted to make a French toast but failed on try? well, search no more, because now you have the opportunity to do one by yourself, following this easy steps!

Ingredients

  • 6 slices of White bread
  • 2 large Eggs
  • 2/3 cup Milk
  • 1 tbsp of Vanilla and 1/4 tbsp ground cinnamon
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 tbsp Butter
  1. Step 1: Whisk milk, eggs, vanilla, cinnamon, and salt together in a shallow bowl.
  2. Step 2: Lightly butter a griddle and heat over medium-high heat.
  3. Step 3: Dunk bread in the egg mixture, soaking both sides. Transfer to the hot skillet and cook until golden, 3 to 4 minutes per side. Serve hot.
