French Toast


Pancakes are easy to make and a great delicious option to serve on dinner or even in breakfast, they are simply soft,sugary, and delicious so, you are gonna defineteley enjoy them!


In this easy-to-follow guide you are gonna learn how to make them without any problem!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  1. Step 1: In a large bowl, mix flour, sugar, baking powder and salt. Make a well in the center, and pour in milk, egg and oil. Mix until smooth.
  2. Step 2: Heat a lightly oiled griddle or frying pan over medium high heat. Pour or scoop the batter onto the griddle, using approximately 1/4 cup for each pancake. Brown on both sides and serve hot.
